Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how FUUNJI Shinjuku uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website (https://f-unji-shinjuku.netlify.app/).

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and improving website performance.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality, such as navigation and security.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve usability and speed.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language or location, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Some cookies may be placed by external services (e.g., analytics, social media) to track performance or allow sharing features.

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website works correctly and securely
  • Improve user experience and site functionality
  • Analyze visitor behavior to enhance our services
  • Enable social media and sharing features

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that some parts of our website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
