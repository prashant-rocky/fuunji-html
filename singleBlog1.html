Discover the Art of Tsukemen at FUUNJI

20 August 2025

At FUUNJI, ramen isn’t just food—it’s a soulful experience. Tucked away in the bustling streets of Shinjuku, our restaurant has become a must-visit destination for ramen lovers from around the world. The secret lies in our meticulous attention to detail: every bowl of tsukemen is crafted with fresh, hand-stretched noodles, paired with a broth that is both rich and deeply flavorful.

From the moment you step inside, the aroma of simmering chicken and seafood broth welcomes you. The atmosphere blends tradition and modern comfort, making each visit memorable. Guests can enjoy perfectly cooked noodles, carefully chosen toppings, and a warm ambiance that invites conversation and relaxation. Watching the chefs at work, one can see the precision and dedication that goes into each bowl—every ladle poured is a promise of quality.

Beyond the taste, FUUNJI offers an experience of discovery. You can experiment with dipping noodles to your preferred richness of broth, savoring every bite at your own pace. Whether it’s your first visit or your tenth, the layers of flavor—from umami-rich stock to tender toppings—ensure that each bowl tells a story. Join us at FUUNJI and see why ramen enthusiasts line up daily for a culinary journey that transcends ordinary noodles.
